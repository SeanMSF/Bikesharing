
# Bikesharing

Overview of the statistical analysis:

Results:  

Men subscribe to bike rentals more often than women and both genders enjoy peak ride times in the 5 o'clock hour as well as leaving their bikes right along the river. 

Summary:  

This analysis will help determine repair times, particularly over night as well as volumes of bikes to rent make available. It also determines targets to market to- men seem to prefer to ride and prescribe!
